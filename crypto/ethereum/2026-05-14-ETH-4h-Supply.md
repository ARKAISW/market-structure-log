ETH has shifted back into a short-term bullish structure with higher lows forming after the recent correction.

Recent candles show improving bullish momentum as price expands upward from local demand.

- **Supply Interaction:**  
Price is now approaching a significant supply region that previously triggered strong rejection.

- **EMA Structure:**  
Short-term EMAs are beginning to realign bullishly as price reclaims dynamic resistance levels.

- **RSI Behavior:**  
RSI is recovering from weaker momentum conditions and moving back toward bullish territory.

## Hypothesis

Current structure favors continued bullish pressure while ETH remains above local support.

### Scenario 1 — Bullish Continuation
If ETH breaks and sustains above supply, continuation toward higher resistance levels becomes likely.

### Scenario 2 — Local Rejection
Failure to break supply may trigger a short-term pullback or consolidation before another continuation attempt.
