# Ethereum — Retracement Into Fibonacci Zone After Demand Reaction



**Date:** 2026-03-30  
**Timeframe:** 4H  
**Instrument:** ETHUSD  

![ETHUSD 4H Chart](../../images/ETHUSD_2026-03-30_22-54-38_0a94a.png)

---

## Context

Ethereum reacted from the demand zone and moved upward, forming a retracement into the Fibonacci zone. Price is now interacting with the 0.382–0.5 Fibonacci region, which often acts as a decision area.

---

## Observation

### 1️⃣ Demand Reaction
- Price previously touched the demand zone and bounced upward.
- This created a short-term bullish retracement.

### 2️⃣ Fibonacci Retracement Zone
- Price is now in the 0.382–0.5 Fibonacci region.
- This area often acts as resistance during bearish conditions.

### 3️⃣ Market Structure
- Overall structure still appears bearish on this timeframe.
- Current move looks like a retracement, not a full trend reversal.

### 4️⃣ RSI
- RSI recovering from lower levels and now around mid-range (~44–51).
- Indicates recovery in momentum but not strong bullish momentum yet.

---

## Hypothesis

### Scenario A — Bearish Continuation
If price gets rejected from the Fibonacci zone, ETH may continue downward toward demand again.

### Scenario B — Bullish Break
If price breaks and holds above the 0.5 Fibonacci level, ETH may move toward the supply zone.

---

## Invalidation / Confirmation

- Rejection from 0.5 Fib → bearish continuation.
- Break above 0.5 Fib → bullish continuation.

---

## Notes

This is a classic retracement-to-Fibonacci setup where price reacts to key fib levels before deciding the next directional move.
