## Context

Ethereum remains within a broader bearish trend after experiencing a severe multi-day selloff. Following the capitulation move into major demand, buyers finally stepped in, generating a strong relief rally from the lows.

Despite the recovery, price continues to trade beneath major higher-timeframe resistance and remains structurally bearish on the 4H timeframe.

* Price formed a clear base after the capitulation low.
* Buyers aggressively defended the demand region.
* Consecutive bullish candles produced the strongest recovery seen since the breakdown.

The demand zone is currently being respected.

### 2️⃣ Relief Rally Structure

* Ethereum has rallied significantly from recent lows.
* The move retraced a portion of the prior bearish impulse.
* Price is now approaching the 0.5 retracement region of the recent decline.

This suggests the current move is corrective until proven otherwise.

### 3️⃣ EMA Resistance Overhead

* Price reclaimed the fastest EMA levels during the rally.
* Higher EMAs remain overhead and continue sloping downward.
* The broader EMA structure remains bearish despite short-term improvement.

Dynamic resistance remains the primary obstacle for bulls.

### 4️⃣ RSI Recovery

* RSI rebounded sharply from oversold territory.
* Momentum has improved substantially compared to the capitulation phase.
* However, RSI remains below levels typically associated with strong bullish trends.

Momentum recovery is visible but not yet decisive.

### 5️⃣ Market Structure

* Lower highs and lower lows remain intact on the higher timeframe.
* No major bullish structure break has occurred.
* Current price action resembles a relief rally within a larger downtrend.

The broader trend remains bearish until key resistance levels are reclaimed.

## Hypothesis

Ethereum is experiencing a corrective recovery after a capitulation event while still operating inside a larger bearish market structure.

Two conditional paths remain active:

### Scenario A — Recovery Continuation

Sustained strength above recent lows and acceptance through the 0.5 retracement zone could extend the rally toward higher liquidity and EMA resistance overhead.

### Scenario B — Bearish Trend Resumption

Failure near current resistance followed by a lower high formation would reinforce the broader downtrend and increase the probability of another move toward demand.

Until higher-timeframe resistance is reclaimed, the current rally should be viewed as corrective.

---

## Invalidation / Confirmation

* Break above major EMA resistance and recent swing highs → bearish thesis weakens.
* Rejection near retracement resistance followed by a lower high → bearish continuation confirmed.
* Higher low formation above demand → recovery structure remains valid.
