## Context

Bitcoin has shown a strong bullish expansion over the past sessions, pushing price into higher levels with sustained upward momentum. The move appears impulsive, indicating strong buying pressure in the short term.

---

## Observation

- **Market Structure:**  
  Clear bullish structure with higher highs and higher lows, confirming short-term trend continuation.

- **Bullish Expansion:**  
  Price moved impulsively upward, breaking out of prior consolidation and establishing new local highs.

- **Current Behavior:**  
  Price is now consolidating near the highs, showing reduced momentum after the strong move.

- **Momentum (RSI):**  
  RSI is elevated and beginning to flatten, indicating potential short-term exhaustion.

---

## Hypothesis

The market is in a **bullish phase with signs of short-term exhaustion**.

Two conditional paths:

### Scenario 1 — Pullback / Reset
If momentum continues to weaken, a short-term pullback or consolidation is likely before the next bullish move.

### Scenario 2 — Immediate Continuation
If price holds near highs and maintains momentum, continuation upward may occur without a deeper retracement.

---

## Invalidation / Failure Mode

- Breakdown below recent higher low  
- Loss of bullish structure  
- RSI dropping below midline with sustained bearish momentum
