Higher lows continue forming after the recent selloff, showing improving bullish structure.

- **Support Reaction:**  
The yellow support region held successfully, allowing buyers to regain short-term control.

- **Momentum:**  
Bullish momentum is strengthening as price climbs back above short-term moving averages.

- **Supply Zone:**  
Price is now approaching a major supply region near previous rejection levels.

- **RSI Behavior:**  
RSI is recovering from weaker momentum and moving back toward bullish territory.

---

## Hypothesis

The current structure favors **continued bullish momentum** unless price faces strong rejection from supply.

### Scenario 1 — Bullish Continuation
If BTC sustains above local support and breaks supply cleanly, expansion toward higher resistance becomes likely.

### Scenario 2 — Supply Rejection
Failure to break the supply zone may trigger another short-term retracement into support before continuation.

- Breakdown below yellow support region  
- Loss of bullish EMA structure
