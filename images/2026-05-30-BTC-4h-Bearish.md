## Context

Bitcoin continues trading within a broader bearish structure after rejection from higher timeframe supply.

### 1️⃣ Supply Rejection Confirmed

* Price failed to reclaim the 4H supply zone overhead.
* Multiple rejection attempts occurred beneath resistance.
* Sellers maintained control throughout the retracement.

The supply region continues acting as a major barrier to upside movement.

### 2️⃣ EMA Alignment

* EMAs remain bearishly stacked and sloping downward.
* Price is trading below the entire EMA cluster.
* Recent bounce attempts were rejected near dynamic resistance.

This confirms continuation of short-term bearish momentum.

### 3️⃣ Fibonacci Structure

* Price retraced only into the 0.5 region before rejection.
* No meaningful acceptance above retracement resistance occurred.
* Current positioning remains closer to the impulse low than the retracement high.

The recovery appears corrective rather than trend-changing.

### 4️⃣ Momentum Conditions

* RSI remains near oversold territory around the low-30 region.
* Momentum has stabilized but has not produced a strong bullish reversal signal.
* Recent candles show consolidation rather than accumulation.

This suggests sellers remain in control despite temporary slowing.

## Hypothesis

Market structure continues to favor bearish continuation while price remains below supply and EMA resistance.

Two conditional paths remain active:

### Scenario A — Continuation Lower

Loss of current support and acceptance below recent lows could trigger another downside leg toward deeper liquidity levels.

### Scenario B — Relief Rally

Short-term oversold conditions may produce a corrective bounce toward the EMA cluster. However, any rally that fails beneath resistance would likely be viewed as a continuation setup.

Until higher resistance is reclaimed, the broader 4H structure remains bearish.
